About

Academic & Research Excellence

Dr. Shanmugadas, Assistant Professor at IIT Jammu, leads the Propulsion and Energy Lab. His expertise includes combustion, two-phase flow, and laser diagnostics for advanced propulsion systems.

Advanced Propulsion Research

Focused on developing multi-fuel gas turbine combustors (Hydrogen, Methane, SAF) and liquid rocket engine technologies with strong emphasis on efficiency and low emissions.

Global Collaborations

Collaborates with ISRO, GE Aerospace, DRDO, IIT Madras (NCCRD), and Technical University of Munich, driving impactful aerospace innovations and research.

Innovation & Leadership

Founder of Machfly Aerospace, working on UCAV and engine technologies. Ex.Associate Dean (Academic Affairs) at IIT Jammu and active contributor to aerospace innovation ecosystem.

Research: Propulsion, Combustion, Clean Energy

Education: B.Tech (Calicut), MS & PhD (IIT Madras)

Projects & Work

Advancing aerospace research through propulsion systems, combustion science, and high-speed flow experiments.

01

Gas Turbine Combustor Development

Development of multi-fuel combustors (Hydrogen, Methane, SAF) focusing on lean combustion, low emissions, and high efficiency using advanced laser diagnostic techniques.

Combustion Clean Energy
02

Liquid Rocket Engine Research

Development of METHALOX pintle injector systems, analyzing spray characteristics, combustion stability, and injector performance for reusable rocket engines.

Rocket Propulsion Injection Systems
03

Supersonic Flow & Atomization (ISRO)

Investigation of jet in supersonic crossflow (M=3), shock structures, and droplet breakup using Schlieren imaging, TR-PIV, and laser diagnostics techniques.

Supersonic Flow ISRO
04

UAV & Aero Engine Innovation (Machfly aerospace )

Development of UCAV propulsion systems, UAV platforms, and integrated avionics with focus on next-generation aerospace applications.
